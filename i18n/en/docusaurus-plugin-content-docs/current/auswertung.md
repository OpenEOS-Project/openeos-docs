# Reports

**Reports** gives you an overview of revenue, orders, and product analytics. Use it to evaluate your event — both during operations and for post-event accounting.

![Reports](/img/screens/en/reports.png)

## Selecting a Period and Event

At the top you filter the report by **Event** and **Period**. Quick-select options **Today**, **Yesterday**, and **All time** are available; alternatively, choose a custom **From** and **To** date.

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Total revenue** | Revenue from paid orders |
| **Orders** | Number of orders / items sold |
| **Avg. order value** | Average per paid order |
| **Deposit (balance)** | Deposit collected minus deposit refunded |

## Detailed Reports

- **Top products** – your highest-revenue products, sorted by revenue.
- **Payment methods** – breakdown of revenue by payment method.
- **Hourly trend** – revenue per hour (0–23), ideal for identifying peak times.

## Export

Each detailed report can be downloaded via **Export CSV**. This lets you process the data further in Excel or your accounting software.

:::tip[Post-event settlement]
After the event, select the **All time** period and export top products and payment methods as CSV — this simplifies the final settlement and the summary report for the board.
:::
