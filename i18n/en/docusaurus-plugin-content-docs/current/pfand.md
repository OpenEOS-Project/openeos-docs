# Deposit

The **Deposit** module lets you charge a deposit on cups, bottles, or crockery. You define deposit types, assign them to products, and specify which sale types incur a deposit. At the till, the deposit is added automatically and can be refunded when items are returned.

![Deposit](/img/screens/en/pfand.png)

## When is a deposit charged?

At the top of the page, two toggles let you choose which sale types incur a deposit:

- **Deposit for table service** — guests served at the table pay a deposit.
- **Deposit for counter / takeaway** — a deposit is charged for sales at the counter or to go.

This lets you, for example, charge a deposit only on takeaway sales but not for table service.

## Create a deposit type

Click **Create deposit type** and define a deposit type (e.g. "Reusable cup" with a deposit amount). You can then assign the deposit type to your drinks or crockery products.

![Create deposit type](/img/screens/en/pfand-dialog.png)

## Deposit in operation

- **Issuing:** When a deposit-bearing product is sold, the deposit amount is automatically added to the order.
- **Returns:** When a guest returns cups or bottles, the till refunds the deposit.
- **Reports:** In the [Reports](./auswertung.md) section you can see the deposit balance — i.e. deposits collected versus deposits refunded.

:::info
The deposit balance helps you reconcile collected and refunded deposits at the end of an event.
:::
