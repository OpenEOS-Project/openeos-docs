# Registration & Login

To use OpenEOS, you first create a free account. The registration process guides you through four steps covering your account, personal details, and organisation.

## Creating an account

Go to [app.openeos.de/register](https://app.openeos.de/register). Registration is divided into four steps, shown as a progress indicator at the top: **Account → You → Organisation → Confirmation**.

### Step 1 – Account

Enter your **email address** and a **password**. The password must be at least 8 characters long and contain upper and lower case letters as well as a number. Confirm the password and click **Next**.

![Registration step 1 – Account](/img/screens/en/reg-step1.png)

### Step 2 – About you

Enter your **first name** and **last name**. These details appear later on invoices and in support communications, for example.

![Registration step 2 – About you](/img/screens/en/reg-step2.png)

### Step 3 – Your organisation

Enter the **name of your organisation** (e.g. your club). The name appears later on invoices and receipts and can be changed at any time. This step is optional — you can also create the organisation later.

![Registration step 3 – Organisation](/img/screens/en/reg-step3.png)

### Step 4 – Final check

Review your details. Accept the **terms and conditions and the privacy policy** (required) and optionally the newsletter. Click **Create account**.

![Registration step 4 – Confirmation](/img/screens/en/reg-step4-confirm.png)

## Confirming your email address

After creating your account you will receive a confirmation email. Click the link in it to activate your account. Use **Go to dashboard** to go straight into the application.

![Account created – email confirmation](/img/screens/de/reg-after-submit.png)

:::info
Confirm your email address promptly. Until the account is activated, some features may be restricted.
:::

## Logging in

At [app.openeos.de/login](https://app.openeos.de/login) you log in with your **email address** and **password**. The **Stay logged in** option keeps your session on the device — convenient for a dedicated till, but not recommended on shared devices.

![Login](/img/screens/en/login.png)

## Switching the language

OpenEOS is available in German and English. Use the **globe icon** in the top right to switch between **DE** and **EN**.
