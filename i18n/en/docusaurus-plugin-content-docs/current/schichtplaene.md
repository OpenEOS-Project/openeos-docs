# Shift Plans

With **Shift Plans** you organise the volunteers for your event. You create shifts and assign your team — so everyone knows when and where they are needed.

![Shift plans](/img/screens/en/shifts.png)

## Creating a Shift Plan

1. Click **New shift plan** or **Create first shift plan**.
2. Set up the shifts with time slots and tasks/areas.
3. Assign volunteers to individual shifts.
4. Save the shift plan.

![Create shift plan](/img/screens/en/shifts-dialog.png)

## Team Coordination

Shift plans provide transparency about who is working at which station and when. Combined with [permissions](./mitglieder.md), you can, for example, give a shift supervisor access to the **Shift plans** module only.

:::tip[Plan ahead]
Create shift plans well in advance of the event so all volunteers know their times and you can spot gaps early.
:::
