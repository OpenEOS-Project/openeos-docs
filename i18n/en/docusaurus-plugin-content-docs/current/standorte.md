# Locations

**Production locations** (or just locations) determine where orders are routed — for example to the **kitchen**, **bar**, or **serving station**. This way, a sausage order goes to the grill and a beer order goes to the bar. Combined with printers and displays, locations keep operations running smoothly behind the scenes.

You can reach this section via **Locations** in the sidebar. Locations also relate to the active event.

![Production locations](/img/screens/en/stations.png)

## Create a location

1. Click **Create location**.
2. Enter a name (e.g. "Kitchen", "Grill", "Drinks counter").
3. Assign the relevant products or categories to the location.
4. Save the location.

![Create location](/img/screens/en/stations-dialog.png)

## How locations work with printers and displays

Each location can be linked to a **printer** (for kitchen or service receipts) and/or a **display**. When an order arrives at the till, items are automatically routed to the responsible location and printed or displayed there.

:::tip[Sensible structure]
Map your real serving stations as locations. A clear separation between food and drinks output noticeably speeds up operations.
:::
